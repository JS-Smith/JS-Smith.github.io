Using The Map #1

Select the tracker in the lower right hand corner of your screen to view nearby & sightings of Pokemon. Pokemon that show up will be located around PokeStops & your surrounding area.

Learning Finesse #2

After finding the Pokemon you wish to catch and selecting them, you'll be brought to a new screen. Below, you'll have your Pokeballs at the ready to throw. Hold down the Pokeball until a reticle appears around the Pokemon. When the reticle is small the catch rate is optimal; fling the Pokeball at the Pokemon and you will have caught it. This may take a few tries though, so keep at it!

Start Your Journey #3

Eggs are obtained from PokeStops as drops or from directly purchasing them from the shop. There are three tiers of eggs, 2KM, 5KM, & 10KM which will determine the rarity of the Pokemon that hatches. After acquiring an egg you must place it in an incubator and start walking. Your phone will use the built in pacer to record how far you've traveled until the distance matches the egg's requirements. Once you've done that, you'll be notified that your egg is ready to hatch!


PokeStops appear as blue icons which turn purple after being activated
By selecting one, you can obtain items, EXP, & read its details so long as you're in range
Purple PokeStops are on cooldown, so wait before getting more items
PokeStops with pink petals have Lures on them, attracting Pokemon
The Competition
Gyms
Battling

Along with PokeStops are locations known as Gyms. These are places where you can fight against enemy trainers' Pokemon to claim them for your team. White Gyms are those that are unclaimed by any faction.


After reaching the necessary level requirements, you will be asked to join one of the three teams in Pokemon GO: Valor, Mystic, & Instinct.


Battling & winning against the lineup of Pokemon claims the Gym for your team, whether it be from another one or an unclaimed. By doing this you gain EXP and the Gym gains Prestige. You can build Prestige if your Gym succeeds in defeating enemy trainers or by battling in the Gym yourself.


Gym battles consist of a lineup of consecutive matches against the trainers that put their Pokemon at stake.


Once you have chosen which Pokemon you want to fight with, when prompted, you will enter the arena. Tap on your Pokemon to use basic attacks, which charge your Move Meter in the top left and swipe left or right to dodge incoming retaliation. Once your meter is full, your Pokemon will use their Special Move, so be sure to time it right!

Following Your Progress

There are two types of badges available in Pokemon GO: the ones that you get from completing achievements and those that you earn from catching multiples of a certain type. Achievement badges will give you experience, while Type badges make it easier to catch more Pokemon of that type.

Getting The Goods

Pokeballs

There are 3 types of Pokeballs in Pokemon GO: Regular, Great, & Ultra. These Pokeballs can be obtained either in the shop or from PokeStops at random. The quality of the Pokeball you get from a PokeStop is dependent on your level as a trainer. *Master Balls may come in later updates*

Incense

Incense is an item that can also be acquired from the shop or as a drop from PokeStops, like most other items. Using an incense will attract wild Pokemon to your location as well as increase their spawn rate.

Lure Modules

Lure Modules attract Pokemon and increases their spawn rate just like Incense. However, unlike Incense they can only be applied to PokeStops and instead of just you benefitting from the rewards, everyone around the Stop does as well.

Lucky Eggs

Lucky Eggs double the amount of EXP you earn within 30 minutes and speed up the progress towards your next trainer level.

Even More...

Discover more amazing items that will aid you on your travels as a trainer to catch them all and claim gyms. You already know the basics so far, so what are you waiting for? Get out there!

Grinding Out The Levels

Leveling up as a trainer is not an easy task, which is why there's so many different ways to earn EXP. The best way to quickly level up though is to have a Lucky Egg active and either catch new Pokemon, evolve Pokemon, hatch Pokemon, win Gym battles, or complete achievements.
